about us

Sargi

Sargi is a women's fashion apparel brand based out of gujarat. the brand main agenda is to promote the local and rural craftsmanship. opportunities for the women working in the very far rural areas. it also focues on the earthy nature of india.

The brand name "sargi " has been derived from a ritual performed in india named karwa chauth sargi is the food that's eaten by the woman who fasts through the day.after taking a bath in the early hours of the morning. this food is prepared by the mother-in-law
